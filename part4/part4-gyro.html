企业介绍

腾讯成立于1998年11月，是目前中国领先的互联网综合服务提供商，也是中国服务用户最多的互联网企业之一，打造了中国最大的网络社区，满足数以亿计互联网用户通信社交、资讯、泛娱乐文化体验、电子商务等方面的需求。在2014年BrandZ全球百大最有价值品牌中位居14，价值540亿美元。成立10多年以来，腾讯一直秉承“一切以用户价值为依归”的经营理念，始终处于稳健发展的状态。2004年6月16日，腾讯控股有限公司在香港联交所主板公开上市（股票代号00700），于2014年11月10日市值超过1500亿美元。

About Tencent

Founded in November, 1998, Tencent has grown into China's leading provider of comprehensive Internet services, creating China's largest Internet community to serve the needs of hundreds of millions of Internet users in the new era of social media, always-on information, communications, trans-entertainment experiences, e-commerce and other emerging forms of interaction and culture change. In BrandZ World's 100 Most Valuable Brands 2014, the company ranked 14th with market value of $54 billion. Since its establishment over the last decade, Tencent has maintained steady growth under its strong emphasis on user-centric strategies. On June 16, 2004, Tencent Holdings Limited (SEHK 00700) went public on the main board of the Hong Kong Stock Exchange and is valued at over 150 billion US dollars on November 10, 2014.

通过互联网服务提升人类生活品质是腾讯的使命，成为最受尊敬的互联网企业是腾讯的愿景。腾讯一直积极参与公益事业、努力承担企业社会责任、推动网络文明。

Tencent's long term vision is to become the most respected Internet enterprise with the mission of enhancing the quality of human life through Internet services. In order to fulfill corporate social responsibilities and to promote civil Internet communities, Tencent has been actively participating in public charity programs.

腾讯提供互联网增值服务、网络广告服务和电子商务服务 。通过即时通信工具QQ、移动社交和通信服务微信和WeChat、门户网站腾讯网（QQ.com）、腾讯游戏、社交网络平台QQ空间等中国领先的网络平台，腾讯打造了中国最大的网络社区，满足互联网用户沟通、资讯、娱乐等方面的需求。截至2014年第三季度，QQ的月活跃帐户数达到8.20亿，最高同时在线帐户数达到2.17亿；微信和WeChat的合并月活跃帐户数达4.68亿。腾讯的发展深刻地影响和改变了数以亿计网民的沟通方式和生活习惯，并为中国互联网行业开创了更加广阔的应用前景。

Presently, Tencent is providing value-added Internet, online advertising and e-commerce services. Tencent's leading Internet platforms in China – QQ Instant Messenger (QQ IM), mobile social and communication service Weixin and WeChat, Tencent's web portal QQ.com, Tencent Games, social networking platform Qzone and more – have brought together China's largest Internet community, to meet the various needs of Internet users including communication, information, entertainment, etc. As of the Third quarter of 2014, the active QQ users accounts for QQ IM amounted to 820 million while its peak concurrent users reached 217 million; total active users in Weixin and WeChat added up to 468 million. The development of Tencent has profoundly influenced the ways hundreds of millions of Internet users communicate with one another as well as their lifestyles. It also creates additional possibilities for a wider range of applications for China's Internet industry.

开放战略与成就
